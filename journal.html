Care Instructions

Balinese amber is an organic gemstone — warm, light, and alive. With the right care, your AMBERRA piece will remain luminous for generations.

✦Daily Wear

Remove jewelry before swimming, bathing, or using cleaning products. Dress your jewelry last — amber is sensitive to perfume and hairspray.

◌Cleaning

Gently wipe with a soft, damp cloth and dry immediately. Use lukewarm water with mild soap for deeper cleaning. No ultrasonic cleaners or steam.

◇Storage

Store in the provided pouch or soft-lined box, away from direct sunlight. Keep pieces separate to avoid scratching.

○Silver Settings

Sterling silver naturally oxidises. Polish gently with a silver cloth. Avoid contact with chlorine, bleach, and rubber.

◈Gold Vermeil

18k gold over sterling silver. Avoid abrasive materials and prolonged submersion. Wipe with a dry soft cloth after each wear.

✧Amber Stone

A drop of olive oil on a soft cloth restores amber’s warm glow. Never use acetone or alcohol — they will damage the surface permanently.
