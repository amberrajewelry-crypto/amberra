Our Story

Born Where Earth Meets Eternity

AMBERRA was born from a single belief: that true beauty is never manufactured — it is found. Our Balinese amber travelled forty million years through ancient forests before reaching the hands of our Balinese artisans.

In the sacred workshops of Ubud, each piece is shaped, polished, and blessed under the Bali sky. Every AMBERRA jewel carries the energy of two worlds — the ancient Balinese coast and the spiritual heart of Bali.

We are PT Amberra Group Creations, a Balinese luxury jewelry house dedicated to feminine empowerment through the timeless beauty of natural amber.
